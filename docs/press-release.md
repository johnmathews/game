## MYSTERIOUS NEW VIDEO GAME SET IN SPAARNDAM -- LOCALS AMAZED

**Release Date: COMING SOON**

SPAARNDAM -- A brand new video game is being built right here in our village, and the whole town is buzzing with
excitement.

The game lets you explore a miniature version of Spaarndam, seen from high above like a bird flying over the rooftops.
Players can walk along the streets, cross the bridges, and peek around every corner. But here is the surprising part --
you can go INSIDE the buildings.

"We could not believe it," said one excited witness. "You walk into a building and suddenly you are inside a whole new
world. There are platformer challenges where you jump and climb and dodge. Every building has its own secret."

When players complete a challenge, they earn a piece of a mysterious map. Nobody knows exactly what the full map looks
like yet. But sources close to the project say it leads to something very important -- people from countries all around
the world who are stuck and need rescuing.

"This is not just a game about exploring," a top secret source told The Gazette. "It is about helping people. The map
pieces are the key."

There are also rumors of a multiplayer mode. Two players will be able to explore Spaarndam at the same time and see each
other on screen. They could work together, split up, or race to find map pieces first.

Each player gets to choose their own special player name.

The developer, who wished to remain mysterious, would only say this: "I am building it for two very special players."
Sources say the game was designed for a brother and sister team -- a brave young adventurer named Atlas and his sister
Vienna, a skilled explorer in her own right.

Details remain top secret. But one thing is clear -- there are hidden surprises in this game that nobody has even hinted
at yet.

Stay tuned. Spaarndam will never be the same.
